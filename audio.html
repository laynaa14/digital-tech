Lossless and Lossy Audio

Lossless Audio Files

Examples of Lossless File Types

  • FLAC
  • WAV
  • ALAC
  • WMA Lossless

Why Use Lossless File Formats

Lossless files are also known as "RAW" format. This means that the audio
or image still contains most of it's original data. Having the original data
means that the quality of the media is much higher which means that the
photo or audio will be much clearer.

Lossy Audio Files

Examples of Lossy Type Files

  • OGG
  • MP4
  • MP3

How Does Lossy Compression Work

Lossy compression is the complete opposite of lossless formats. Instead of
a file containing it's original data, specific information is reduced.

Why use Lossy File Formats?

Although having lossless audio is much better and clearer, lossy compression is
helful. Since lossy compression loses certain information, it's file size is smaller
and takes up less space.
