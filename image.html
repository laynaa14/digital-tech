Lossy and Lossless Images

The Difference Between Lossy and Lossless Compression on Images

The main difference between lossy and lossless compression is
the quality of the photo. Lossless images have a higher quality
than lossy images which means that the image is much clearer whilst
lossy images have lower quality which equals to blurrier photos.

Why is it Important for Web Designers to Compress Image Files

The main reasons as to why it is important to compress image files when
making a website is because it takes a longer amount of time to load
when you have a bigger file uploaded.
